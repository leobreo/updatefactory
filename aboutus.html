About me

Welcome to Update Factory

My name is Leonard Brenk and I am a Computer Science Student. I created this website to collect ideas for potential updates for any company. I always wondered why certain apps or companies are obviously lacking a specific feature which would improve the user experience immensly. However, it remained only a thought since there are almost now options for users to express their wished changes. With this website I want to change that. If you have an idea or adaption to any app or servie provided by a company, submit it on the website and I will upload it and make it accessible for everybody!

Lets work together, take it upon ourselves and drive forward change!
