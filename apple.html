Apple - by Leonard Brenk

Imessage Voice Mails

Concerning the voice recording integration imessage has a lot of catching up to do. As soon as the screen rotates the recording stopps and is lost forever. This can be quite frustrating when the user has been recording for some time. Thats why this update suggests either a stop/play tool for recordings or a rotation lock during recording. It shouldn't be too hard to implement and will make it easier and attractive to use imessage seeing that voice memos are a huge part of todays digital conversations.
